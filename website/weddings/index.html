Frequently Asked Questions

How do guests access flick?

You'll receive a unique QR code to include in your wedding communications (save-the-dates, invitations, welcome bags, or table cards). Guests scan it, download the app, and instantly join your private wedding room.

Is it only for single guests?

Yes! flick is designed specifically for single guests who want to connect. We recommend mentioning it in your invitations or welcome materials so singles know it's available.

When can guests start using it?

Access begins as soon as you activate your QR code (typically 2-4 weeks before the wedding). Guests can start matching and chatting before the big day, so they can plan to meet at cocktail hour!

What happens after the wedding?

Guests keep their matches and can continue chatting for 30 days after your wedding. After that, the private room closes. This creates a special window for connections without being a permanent dating app.

Is this appropriate for all ages?

flick is for adults 18+. We maintain a respectful, safe environment with reporting tools and moderation. It's designed to be wedding-appropriate and elegant.

Can we customize it with our wedding theme?

Yes! Your wedding room will display your names and wedding date. Venue/Planner packages include white-label options with custom branding.
